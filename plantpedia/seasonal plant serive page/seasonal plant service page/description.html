Seasonal Plants Overview

Seasonal plants are those that thrive in specific seasons and exhibit distinctive characteristics during those times. Here is a brief overview of plants in different seasons:

Spring Plants

During spring, plants often bloom with vibrant flowers and fresh greenery. Examples include tulips, daffodils, cherry blossoms, and various types of flowering trees.

Summer Plants

Summer plants are known for their ability to withstand heat and often feature bright and bold colors. Sunflowers, roses, zinnias, and herbs like basil and mint are common during this season.

Fall Plants

In the fall, many plants produce rich and warm hues. Examples include pumpkins, mums, marigolds, and trees that showcase beautiful autumn foliage.

Winter Plants

Winter plants may include evergreens like pine and holly, as well as seasonal favorites like poinsettias. These plants often add festive touches during the holiday season.

It's essential to understand the unique needs of seasonal plants and provide appropriate care to ensure their health and vitality throughout the year.
