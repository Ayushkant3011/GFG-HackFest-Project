Precautions for Protecting Seasonal Plants

Insect Protection

  • Regularly inspect plants for signs of insect infestation.

  • Use natural insect repellents or organic pesticides.

  • Encourage natural predators like ladybugs or spiders to control insect populations.

  • Remove affected leaves or plants promptly to prevent the spread of pests.

  • Consider companion planting to deter specific insects.

Weather Protection

  • Provide appropriate shelter during extreme weather conditions.

  • Use mulch to protect plants from temperature extremes and retain moisture.

  • Cover plants during frost or freeze warnings.

  • Water plants adequately, especially during hot and dry periods.

  • Install windbreaks to shield plants from strong winds.
